The Academy

We teach professionals how to build their own digital cortex — and we give them the seed agents to start with. Free. Open source. No coding required.

Why an Academy

AI moves fast. The tools change every month. But the professionals who need them most — lawyers, consultants, researchers, project managers — do not have time to keep up.

Hadosh Academy exists to close that gap. We teach the ideas behind intelligent agents through plain-language essays, and we ship free seed agents anyone can download and start using immediately.

No prerequisites. No coding. Just a professional who wants to work smarter.

From Chatbot to Digital Cortex

Most people use AI as a chatbot — a helpful stranger they visit when they have a question. We teach a different approach. An agent that lives on your computer, learns your workflow, remembers your preferences, and improves every time you use it.

A seed agent is the starting point. You download it, open it in your terminal, and start talking. Over days and weeks, it absorbs your expertise and becomes something no generic chatbot can be — a cognitive extension built around your professional life.

That is what we mean by a digital cortex. And anyone can build one.

What We Offer

Free Seed Agents

Open-source starting templates with memory, rules, and skills built in. Download one, open your terminal, and start customizing it through conversation.

Education
