You Already Know the Problem

Modern professional life is relentlessly complex. Taxes, contracts, client management, scheduling, research, compliance — the list never ends and never slows down.

You use AI already. You open a browser, type a question, get an answer. It helps. But it does not know your work. It does not remember your process. It cannot touch your files, follow your rules, or learn from your mistakes.

You need more than a chatbot. You need an agent that is yours.

What We Build

Seed Agents

Free, open-source agent architectures that work out of the box. Download a seed, open your terminal, start working. The agent comes with a structure — memory, rules, skills — ready to go.

Customize Through Conversation

Tell the agent how you work. It adapts — its memory, its rules, its personality — to match your needs. No coding. Your words reshape the system.

Consistent & Inspectable

Every decision the agent makes is visible. Every rule is a file you can read. Nothing is hidden. It gets better because you can see exactly how it works.

For Every Professional

The Bigger Picture

Your brain was never built for the complexity of modern work. It evolved for a savanna — one threat at a time, immediate and physical. Today you face infinite abstract demands, all day, every day.

CLI agents are the next layer of your brain — a digital cortex. They handle the complexity your biology cannot. They remember what you forget. They enforce the rules you set. They scale your judgment across every project, every client, every deadline.

Hadosh Academy is building the infrastructure for this future. Free. Open source. One seed agent at a time.
