How to read this deck

Walk a job through its whole life.

A single prompt births a seed-agent job — completely empty, no name, no goal. From there it discovers what it is, chooses its shape, builds, checks itself, and consolidates what it learned. Each card here is one focused idea, with a clean diagram you can hover and click.
