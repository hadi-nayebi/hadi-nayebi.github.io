How to read this deck

A phase has to reflect before it can leave.

Every OPEVC phase runs the same shape: it enters with no friction at all, does its work paced by a min-max gate, and can only advance once it proves it reflected — three families of evidence at the exit. Each card here is one focused idea, with a clean diagram you can hover and click.
