How to read this deck

Three events hide behind one word.

When a job is “done”, three different things happen at three different moments — its record completes, it loses focus, and the cycle counter ticks. Pulling them apart is what keeps the seed from deadlocking. Each card here is one focused idea, with a clean diagram you can hover and click.
