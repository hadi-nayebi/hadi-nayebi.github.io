How to read this map

This is one turn of the agent.

Your prompt enters on the left. The agent loops in the middle — thinking, acting, delegating — until it decides to Stop on the right. Then it loops back for your next message.

  1. 1Follow the numbered steps along the bright main line — that is the reading order.
  2. 2Hover any box for a one-line summary; click it for the full story.
  3. 3Click a glowing room (Tool Loop, Delegation) to step inside its sub-flow.
  4. 4Lost? Press Esc or hit fit to snap back to the whole map.

Tools — the action space

Everything the agent can actually do in the world. The model proposes one of these each step; nothing happens until a tool runs. ● needs permission · ● read-only. Click any tool for detail.

The delegation ladder (worth learning): TodoWrite / Task* track the agent's OWN work · Agent delegates ONE side-task to a separate mind · Workflow conducts MANY subagents at once. Naming trap: the old “Task” tool was renamed Agent; today's Task* tools are the to-do system — so “Task” means different things across versions.
